## Frequently Asked Questions

**Q: What should I order at Rome's Kitchen?**
The BBQ hot link sandwich is one of a kind. The fried fish with seasoning is exceptional. The candied yams and cabbage are full of flavor.

**Q: Where can I find Rome's Kitchen?**
They do pop-ups in various locations during the week. Follow their Instagram to stay updated on where they'll be next.

**Q: Is Rome's Kitchen good for catering?**
Yes, they catered fried chicken, mac and cheese, greens, and yams for a work event and everything was delicious, hot, and fresh.

**Q: How's the fish at Rome's Kitchen?**
Outstanding. One reviewer who normally doesn't like fish said Rome's redeemed fish for them. The seasoning on the fried fish is exceptional.

**Q: How's the service?**
Above and beyond. The people are genuinely nice and care about the food they serve. Reviewers consistently praise the warmth and hospitality.
