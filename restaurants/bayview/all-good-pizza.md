## Frequently Asked Questions

**Q: Is All Good Pizza worth trying?**
Opinions are split. Pizza lovers rave about thin crust Neapolitan-style pizza with incredible flavors, but some find it too oily with soggy salads.

**Q: What's the best thing to order at All Good Pizza?**
The meatball sub with incredible meatballs, the mushroom panini, and the Neapolitan-style pizza are standouts. The hibiscus lemonade is also popular.

**Q: What's the atmosphere like at All Good Pizza?**
A funky parking lot setting in southeast San Francisco. Clean, cozy inside with good music selection. It's casual and charming.

**Q: Any downsides to All Good Pizza?**
Some reviewers found excess oil dripping from the pizza. The salad can be soggy. Hours are limited, and they're closed on some days.

**Q: How are the prices at All Good Pizza?**
A sausage pizza runs about $20.50. Not cheap, but the quality and Neapolitan style are what you're paying for.
