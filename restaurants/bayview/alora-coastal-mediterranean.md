## Frequently Asked Questions

**Q: Is Alora good for vegetarians?**
Yes, the vegetarian menu variety is impressive with dishes that are flavorful and beautifully presented. A true gem for vegetarian lovers.

**Q: Is Alora good for happy hour?**
Yes, the happy hour menu features oysters, negronis, and limoncello spritzes. Everything is fresh and well done. A lovely way to start the weekend.

**Q: How's the atmosphere at Alora?**
Fancy-looking spot with a beautiful bar. The atmosphere is great for dates or happy hours. Mediterranean coastal vibes.

**Q: How's the service at Alora?**
Outstanding. The server Pooja was specifically praised. Staff is super friendly and makes the overall experience special.

**Q: Any downsides to Alora?**
Some reviewers found most items slightly overpriced for the portion sizes. The mezze platter is tasty but overall pricing runs on the higher side.
