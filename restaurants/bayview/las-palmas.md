## Frequently Asked Questions

**Q: What's best at Las Palmas?**
The chicken wings and steak & shrimp plate are top picks. The fish and burrito combo is described as soul-filling. Everything is fresh to order.

**Q: How are the portions at Las Palmas?**
Super large portions. The food is described as bomb with great flavors. Good lemonade too.

**Q: Any food safety concerns at Las Palmas?**
One reviewer got food poisoning from chicken and warns about unsafe frying grease. Another found they received a different fish than ordered. Mixed experiences.

**Q: Is the catfish still good at Las Palmas?**
A longtime fan reports the crispy cornmeal battered catfish nuggets have changed. Recently received mushy, flavorless fish that wasn't catfish. Quality may have declined.
