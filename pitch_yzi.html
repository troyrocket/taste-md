Problem

45% of consumers use AI to find restaurants. AI can't find them.

Consumers get hallucinated answers. Restaurants are invisible to AI. And AI can't recommend well — because today's internet was built for human eyes, not agents.

Yelp's robots.txt blocks every major AI crawler — GPTBot, ClaudeBot, PerplexityBot. Not ignorance. Structural conflict of interest.

Why Markdown

The universal language of AI.

  • 80% fewer tokens — fraction of inference cost
  • Structure = cognition — headings and lists are semantic maps
  • Human + machine readable — one file serves both
  • Zero friction — no JS, no login walls

Same page: HTML 16,180 tokens. Markdown 3,150 tokens.
